THE PROBLEM

Selling used dorm stuff today is risky, awkward, and a total mess.

💸
Tight budgets, risky buys
Buying used feels like a gamble — no way to know if the seller is honest or the item's a dud.
😳
Too much effort, too awkward
Selling takes forever, and students feel embarrassed to post publicly or haggle face-to-face.
📱
Messy WhatsApp & FB groups
Chat groups are unstructured, unsafe, and impossible to search. Good deals vanish in the scroll.
WHAT MAKES LOKITA DIFFERENT

Security a generic app can't match — built for campus trust.

Five features designed around one idea: everyone here is a verified neighbour.

FEATURE 01
🛡️

Dorm-Verified
Identity System

Every user signs up with a university email and exact dorm room — like Thomas House T108. A dorm-wide rating exposes dishonest sellers, so social accountability stops bad behaviour before it starts.

✓ Real names · Real rooms · Real reviews
FEATURE 02
🏛️

No meetings needed

Buyer pays in-app first. Seller drops the item at the campus Security Post or Dorm Office. Buyer picks it up on their own time — no scheduling, no awkward meetups, no conflicts.

FEATURE 03
🎓

Graduation Package

Seniors sell an entire room — bed, fan, desk — as one big bundle. Move out fast, hand your setup to the next student.

FEATURE 04
🙋

Wanted requests

Can't find it? Post a "Wanted" request and sellers respond fast — turning idle stuff into quick deals.

FEATURE 05
💬

Reviews & simple chat

Post-transaction feedback stays visible to your dorm community, and in-app messaging keeps quick negotiations easy and on the record.

HASSLE-FREE EXCHANGE

Three steps. Zero awkward meetups.

1
Buyer pays in-app
Money is held safely in escrow — the seller knows it's a real, committed buyer.
2
Seller drops it off
At the campus Security Post or Dorm Office — a place both people already trust.
3
Buyer picks it up
Whenever it's convenient. Payment releases to the seller. Everyone's happy.
